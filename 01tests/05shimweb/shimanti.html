ABOUT ME

I love to learn

I'm a newbie in the world of computer science, but love to learn. I have taken all kinds of classes over the years, some for work, like pusuing my MBA in a joint Executive program with UC Berkeley and Columbia University. I call it two degrees for twice the price! Lately, I've also pursued fields of interest, including photography, cooking, and art. Sadly, none of those lessons have stuck with me, and I remain an amateur in all those fields. However, my latest passion is learning how to build a mobile application. My curiosity has led me to learn more about computer science and, somehow, I ended up auditing the incredible Harvard CS50x taught by David Malan. I feel like I'm in heaven learning the theory that links all the fields of computer science together. I have also tried to crash the Harvard CS50 Beyond class, but have learned, the hard way, that I need to complete CS50 before coding with the adults. In spite of that, I'm almost through both the CS50x and CS50 Beyond lectures, which means I only have the actual assignments left to complete... that'll be easy, right?!
