Recipe: Easy One-Pot Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb linguine (whole wheat or regular)
  • 1 lb grape tomatoes, halved
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced (reduce crying by cutting and adding last)
  • 5 cloves garlic, crushed or thinly sliced
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 2 tsp dried basil
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 5 cups water
  • Freshly grated parmesan cheese
  • Extra olive oil, salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Combine pasta, tomatoes, onion, garlic, red pepper flakes, dried basil, olive oil, salt, pepper, water in a big pot.
  2. Keeping pasta on the bottom, mix the other ingredients.
  3. Boil until pasta is al dente (about 8-10mins).
  4. Mix all ingredients.
  5. Serve in bowls. Add (at least 1 tsp) parmesan, to taste.
  6. Add olive oil, freshly ground salt and pepper, to taste.
